About

Pujan is a PhD candidate in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Ronald K. Hanson, working on optical diagnostics and shock-tube experiments to study combustion and extreme environments. [file:22]

His research integrates laser absorption spectroscopy, dual-comb techniques, and data-driven modeling to obtain quantitative insight into fuel chemistry, plasma processes, and high-temperature flows relevant to cleaner energy. [file:21][file:22]
